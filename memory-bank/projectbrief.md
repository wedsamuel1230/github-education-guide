# Project Brief: VTC Student Benefits Research

## Objective
Comprehensive research and documentation of free and discounted software tools, services, and resources available to Hong Kong Vocational Training Council (VTC) students across multiple institutions (IVE, THEi, PEAK, etc.).

## Target Audience
VTC students pursing higher diploma, diploma, and post-secondary courses seeking cost-effective professional development tools.

## Key Deliverable
Markdown summary document compiling all available student benefits, organized by category (Development, Design, Cloud, Collaboration, ML/Data, etc.)

## Constraints
- Research based on public educational programs (GitHub Student Pack, JetBrains Education, etc.)
- VTC students must have valid institutional affiliation
- Most benefits require annual renewal

## Definition of Done
✅ Comprehensive markdown file created
✅ All research findings compiled
✅ Categories clearly organized
✅ Actionable guidance provided
